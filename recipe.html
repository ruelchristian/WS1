Fruit and Veggies Smoothies

Mango Spinach Delight

A tropical blend of sweet mango and spinach for a nutritious, refreshing drink that balances taste and health.

  • 1 ripe Philippine Mango (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 cup Spinach leaves
  • 1 Banana
  • 1 cup Coconut Water
  • 1 tsp Honey (optional)

Guyabano Kale Energizer

This smoothie offers a combination of the tart flavor of guyabano and the nutritious kale, sweetened with pineapple.

  • 1 cup Guyabano (Soursop) pulp (seeds removed)
  • 1 cup Kale leaves
  • 1/2 cup Pineapple chunks
  • 1 tsp Honey
  • 1 cup Cold Water or Ice

Calamansi Cucumber Cooler

A refreshing, tangy smoothie that combines the zesty flavor of calamansi and the coolness of cucumber, with a hint of mint.

  • 5-6 Calamansi (juiced)
  • 1 Cucumber (peeled and chopped)
  • 5-6 Mint Leaves
  • 1 cup Coconut Water
  • 1 tsp Honey (optional)

Papaya Carrot Boost

This vibrant orange smoothie blends the sweetness of papaya and the nutritional power of carrots, with a zing from ginger.

  • 1 cup Papaya (peeled and chopped)
  • 1/2 Carrot (chopped)
  • 1 tsp fresh Ginger (grated)
  • 1/2 cup Orange Juice
  • 1/2 cup Water or Ice

Pineapple Malunggay Power

A nutrient-dense smoothie featuring tropical pineapple and the superfood malunggay, creating a perfect balance of flavor and health benefits.

  • 1 cup Pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup Malunggay (Moringa) leaves
  • 1 small Apple (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 cup Water

Banana Avocado Cream Smoothie

This creamy smoothie is a blend of banana and avocado, creating a rich, satisfying drink packed with healthy fats.

  • 1 Banana
  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 1 cup Almond Milk
  • 1 tsp Honey
  • 1/2 cup Ice

Watermelon Basil Hydration Smoothie

Hydrate and refresh with this light and cooling watermelon-basil smoothie, perfect for hot days or after a workout.

  • 1 1/2 cups Watermelon chunks
  • 5-6 Basil leaves
  • 1 cup Coconut Water
  • 1 tbsp Lime Juice

Dragonfruit Cucumber Detox

A vibrant detox smoothie combining dragonfruit's bold flavor with the fresh, cooling taste of cucumber and spinach.

  • 1/2 Dragonfruit (peeled and chopped)
  • 1/2 Cucumber (peeled and chopped)
  • 1 cup Spinach leaves
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
  • 1 cup Water or Ice

Avocado Mango Green Smoothie

A creamy, green smoothie that blends the rich textures of avocado and mango, powered by kale and sweetened with coconut milk.

  • 1/2 Avocado
  • 1 cup Philippine Mango chunks
  • 1/2 cup Kale leaves
  • 1 cup Coconut Milk
  • 1 tsp Honey (optional)

Pomelo Carrot Immunity Booster

Packed with immunity-boosting ingredients, this smoothie pairs the tangy taste of pomelo with the earthy flavors of carrot, ginger, and turmeric.

  • 1/2 Pomelo (peeled and sectioned)
  • 1/2 Carrot (chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp fresh Ginger (grated)
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup Orange Juice
  • 1/2 cup Water or Ice
